Word Class Events

The Country Club offers the most sophisticated spaces for hosting meetings, conferences, conventions, symposiums, banquets and performances with state of the art facilities and amenities. In fact, the facilities available at The Country Club, which include meeting rooms, banquet halls and conference rooms, are one of the largest and most comprehensive in Vancouver. Main Hall, with its enormous pillarless area, can be divided into 8 separate spaces with its own private pre-function area. On the other hand, the banquet area has its own exclusive entrance.

More Amenities

  • Book Golf Carts.

    • Booking golf carts on spot is available for members, but depending on its availability.

  • Buy/Book Golf Requisites.

    • Booking and buy options available for golf products that members need to play in court. This include: golf clubs, balls or any other necessities required.

  • Buy/Book Tennis Requisites.

    • Booking and buy options available for tennis products such as tennis racket, tennis ball, shoes or any other necessities required.

  • Personal Trainers.

    • Personal Trainers can be availed for full or junior members for a monthly fee. Time slots with trainer should be pre-booked.

  • Car Parking.

    • Ample options for car parking for members. Non-members cannot park their vehicle for more than an hour.

  • 24/7 Security

    • Our club provides security 24/7. Anything unusual should be reported immediately.
